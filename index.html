Meet Our Founder

Talicia Rodriguez, Founder & CEO

Talicia founded TaliCare & Co. to bring the same compassion, patience, and hands-on skill she's practiced for 14+ years as a CNA directly into the homes of Springfield-area families.

What started as a passion for one-on-one caregiving grew into a mission: build care plans around each client's real life, not a one-size-fits-all schedule — and connect families to every resource they need, whether or not it's covered by insurance.
